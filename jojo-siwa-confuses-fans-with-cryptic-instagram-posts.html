JoJo Siwa left fans confused earlier this week when she posted a series of cryptic images on Instagram. One photo featured her flipping off the camera, and the captions appeared to spell out a message about karma.

JoJo Siwa Shares Cryptic Instagram Posts About Karma

The photos Siwa posted to Instagram mostly showed her in the studio with other dancers. However, there was one shot of her giving a peace sign and sticking out her tongue, and another of her holding up two middle fingers.

Arguably the most confusing part of the posts was the fact that each photo was accompanied by a single word which seemed to spell out a message: “See you in one month remember Karma’s a b*tch.”

Fans were left wondering about the meaning of the photos, as well as the message. Some speculated that Siwa was entering a darker, more mature new era. One fan called it her “f*ck it era.” Another compared it to Miley Cyrus’ Bangerz era.

Other commenters seemed to show concern for the star, with one writing, “Is JOJO BROKEN?” and another wondering, “What is going on jojo r u ok.” Others are simply anticipating a new song or performance next month.

Siwa Is Judging So You Think You Can Dance

Siwa will return as a judge on Season 18 of So You Think You Can Dance, alongside Maksim Chmerkovskiy and Allison Holker. Siwa previously judged the show in Season 17. This time, she’s returning to the panel as a replacement for Nigel Lythgoe, who left the show amid sexual assault accusations.

The season premiered on March 4, with new episodes airing Monday nights on FOX. For the audition round, former SYTYCD star Comfort Fedoke joined Chmerkovskiy and Holker on the panel. Fans will have to wait a little longer to see Siwa on the show.
