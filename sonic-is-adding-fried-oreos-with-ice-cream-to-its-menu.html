Sonic is adding fried Oreos with ice cream to its menu

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Next week, the drive-in fast-food chain will start selling fried Oreos with ice cream, a Sonic representative told Best Products. Each order comes with three Oreos.

The Fried Oreo A La Mode will be released on January 29

Shutterstock

ADVERTISEMENT

The new Fried Oreo A La Mode will be added on January 29 at participating locations and will cost $2.99.

The treat is "perfectly battered and fried to create a delectably crunchy and dippable treat served with SONICs famous Real Ice Cream," the Oklahoma City-based restaurant described in a press release sent to the outlet.

Sonic is also releasing a Cinnabon Cinnasnacks A La Mode next week

But the state fair staple isnt the only addition to the Sonic dessert menu. Theyll also be releasing Cinnabon Cinnasnacks A La Mode, which is described as a "warm, buttery pastries filled with melt-in-your-mouth cinnamon roll filling served with SONICs Real Ice Cream."

ADVERTISEMENT

And Sonic's been on a new-options streak, earlier this year Sonic also added a savory option to their menu: Hearty Chili Bowls , giving Sonic's chili fans a chance to eat it in snack, side dish, or as its own meal.

It's unclear if these additions will be on the permanent menu or if they're available for a limited time.
