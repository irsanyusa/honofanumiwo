People Are Curious Why Kim Kardashian Wore a Cardigan With Her Sheer Corset Dress to the Met Gala

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 1, 2024

Kim Kardashian captured the spotlight at the Met Gala with her striking appearance. The television personality and businesswoman made a bold statement in a dazzling dress, which sparked conversations among her admirers, particularly regarding her extremely slender waistline and the odd pairing of sweater.

Kim Kardashian’s Met Gala Appearance

ANGELA WEISS/AFP/East News, ANGELA WEISS/AFP/East News

At 43, Kardashian chose a sophisticated John Galliano creation for Maison Margiela, which featured a design that significantly highlighted her waist.

Her outfit was a fusion of a shimmering corset dress and a translucent maxi skirt embellished with subtle floral designs. The outfit’s design emphasized her narrow waist, which was a standout feature in her form-fitting attire.

The Fashion Statement: Combining a Cardigan with a Sheer Corset Dress

Kim Kardashian, sporting her new platinum blonde hair arranged in a casual mermaid braid, surprised many by pairing a simple gray sweater with her glittering corset dress at the Met Gala, held at the Metropolitan Museum of Art.

The decision to wear the sweater was a deliberate one, with Kim explaining that it was a playful nod to the event’s theme of the year. Kim Kardashian shared that her Met Gala look was inspired by a spontaneous moment. She aimed to capture the essence of a hurried departure following an eventful night in a garden. With a chuckle, she described how she swiftly grabbed her boyfriend’s sweater to wear over her dress and set off for her day, her hair disheveled from the night’s adventures.

The Met Gala’s theme this year was “The Garden of Time,” complementing the Costume Institute’s exhibit “Sleeping Beauties: Reawakening Fashion.” Kardashian’s choice of attire was a creative interpretation of the gala’s dress code, blending casual comfort with high fashion. The choice was also in line with Maison Margiela’s recent Artisanal collection, where similar sweaters were featured.

Fashion experts and designer John Galliano himself have noted that the sweater’s inclusion was intentional and consistent with the style presented in the collection. The casual cardigan juxtaposed with the couture corset dress created a unique and memorable ensemble that sparked discussions and left a lasting impression on the fashion world.

Designer Insights: Who Created Kim’s Gala Outfit?

Kim Kardashian’s 2024 Met Gala outfit was created by John Galliano for Maison Margiela. John Galliano is a British fashion designer who has led his own label and French fashion houses Givenchy and Dior. Currently, he’s the creative director of Maison Margiela.

He’s known for his theatrical and feminine designs, with one of his most famous pieces being the gown worn by Nicole Kidman at the 1997 Academy Awards. He’s been named British Designer of the Year four times and was listed as the fifth most influential person in British culture in a 2004 BBC poll. Kim’s Met Gala look was meant to reflect the “wildest night” through her deliberately “messed up” hair.

Public Reaction to Kim Kardashian’s Outfit Choice

Kim Kardashian’s latest fashion choice has garnered widespread attention, with many admirers lauding her for her daring and stylish ensemble, calling her “iconic” and “so beautiful.”

Conversely, there was a notable number of individuals who voiced their unease regarding the snug fit around Kim’s waist, suggesting it might be causing her discomfort. Comments ranged from concerned remarks like “Girl, you look in so much pain,” to queries about her physical well-being, such as “Girl, where are your organs?” Additionally, some criticized her fashion decision, bluntly labeling it as “Totally obscene!”

Behind the Scenes: Preparing for the Met Gala

Kim Kardashian’s preparation for the Met Gala 2024 was quite extensive. She collaborated with John Galliano to create a dress symbolizing “the day after the greatest night of her life.” She opted for “floating heels” to complete the look, she shared in behind-the-scene photos.

The choice of floating heels was a blend of innovation and practicality. The heelless, clear lucite shoes were specifically designed to avoid interference with the gown’s train, which could potentially lead to damage or a fall. Kim Kardashian likened the experience of wearing these shoes to that of a ballerina poised on her toes, a careful measure to preserve the integrity of her outfit while aligning with the gala’s theme.
