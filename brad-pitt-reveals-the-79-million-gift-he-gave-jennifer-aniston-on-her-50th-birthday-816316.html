Brad Pitt Reveals the $79 Million Gift He Gave Jennifer Aniston on Her 50th Birthday

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 15, 2024

Brad Pitt reportedly gave Jennifer Aniston a heartfelt gift for her birthday, marking a meaningful moment in their ever-evolving relationship. Remember when they were a famous Hollywood couple who got hitched in that unforgettable 2000 wedding? It’s pretty surprising how time can change things between people.

They wrapped up their marriage in a peaceful manner.

Khayat Nicolas/ABACA/EAST NEWS

The story of Jennifer Aniston and Brad Pitt’s relationship is a captivating tale of Hollywood romance, beginning in 1994 when they first crossed paths thanks to their managers’ friendship. Aniston, reflecting on those early days, described Pitt as a “sweet guy from Missouri.” The two had an immediate connection, culminating in their marriage in 2000, symbolizing the union of these two beloved Hollywood stars.

Their story, however, took a turn for the unexpected when they decided to part ways in 2005. In a joint statement, they emphasized that the reason for their breakup wasn’t the fodder for speculation that tabloids churned out. Instead, it was a conscious and amicable decision. They wanted the world to know that they still cared deeply for one another and held each other in high regard, despite the end of their romantic relationship.

However, not long after their separation, Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie’s public appearances together added another layer of complexity to the narrative. Aniston admitted that dealing with this development was challenging. However, both Pitt and Aniston chose to take the high road and refrained from making any disrespectful comments about each other, either in the immediate aftermath or in the years that followed.

Brad and Jennifer have reconnected after a few years.

After they split, Jennifer Aniston said: “I really do hope that someday we can be friends again.” She said. “I will love [Brad] for the rest of my life. He’s a fantastic man. I don’t regret any of it, and I’m not going to beat myself up about it.” As the years went by, it looked like her wish had become a reality.

In a heartwarming gesture, Brad Pitt even attended Jennifer Aniston’s 50th birthday celebration, but the peak of their rekindled friendship happened in 2020 at the 26th Annual Screen Actors Guild Awards. They genuinely seemed happy for each other. Jennifer Aniston was seen laughing and applauding Brad Pitt’s speech while he watched her win an award from backstage.

And after a while, Jennifer herself confirmed the status of their relationship, saying, “Brad and I are buddies; we’re friends,” Aniston said. “And we speak and there’s no oddness at all, except for everyone that probably watched it and was wanting there to be, or assumed there to be.”

He gave her a dream gift on her birthday.

Brad Pitt is not a typical ex-husband. In a move that has left many in awe, the Hollywood actor has performed an incredibly sweet and extravagant gesture for Jennifer Aniston. The facts date back to 2019. According to reports, Brad spent a jaw-dropping $79 million to reacquire the Beverly Hills mansion they once shared during their marriage, only to present it to Jennifer as a surprise birthday gift on her 50th birthday.

The house, which held sentimental value as it was the place where they had once built their life together, was originally acquired in 2001, shortly after their marriage, for $13.1 million. However, following their divorce in 2005, Brad took possession of the property. When their marriage ended, it was reported that Jennifer Aniston regretted not buying Brad out of their shared dream home, and it became a poignant symbol of their separation.

Jennifer said that “losing their dream home worsened the heartache of their divorce, and it was one of her biggest regrets...”. But as fate would have it, Brad’s generous spirit stepped in to heal the old wounds and the decision to buy the house back is a grand gesture that transcends the boundaries of ordinary post-divorce interactions.

On the other hand, their rekindled friendship in recent years might have served as a catalyst for Brad’s grand gift, signaling that bygones are truly bygones. The idea of spending $79 million on a mansion to make an ex-spouse happy does appear extravagant by any standards. Even for someone with Brad Pitt’s estimated net worth of $400 million, it’s a remarkable display of generosity.

What was Jennifer’s reaction to the gift?

Collin Xavier/Image Press Agency ABACA/Abaca/East News, Invision/Invision/East News

When Jennifer learned about Brad Pitt’s plans, she was reportedly quite touched, “Jen was speechless”, a source said. With Brad’s extravagant birthday gift to Jennifer Aniston, there’s been a lot of talk about what it really means. The lavish mansion with 5 bedrooms, 12 bathrooms, a cinema room, a lounge, a tennis court, and even lodges for friends could easily be seen as a grand gesture to win her back.

Jennifer, post-divorce from Justin Theroux, has been relatively low-key when it comes to her dating life, and there have been no significant rumors about her dating anyone new. In contrast, Brad Pitt has been in the news for his connections with various women, like Emily Ratajkowski, and he’s been linked to Ines de Ramon for about a year.

So, the public’s curiosity is piqued about what’s going on between Brad and Jen. Is this gift just a friendly gesture or something more? We’ll have to wait and see how this Hollywood story unfolds.

Jennifer and Brad’s ongoing connection never fails to pique people’s curiosity, even after all these years. On the flip side, Brad Pitt’s love life has been notably turbulent, partly due to the way he’s conducted his relationships. Some might even speculate that he’s adapted his style to match that of the women he’s dated.
