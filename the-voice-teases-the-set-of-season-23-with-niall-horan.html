'The Voice' Teases The Set of Season 23 With Niall Horan

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, January 1, 0001

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing The Voice

Photo by Gareth Cattermole/Getty Images, The Voice via Twitter

Reading Time: 2 minutes

The Voice has given the fans a reason to be excited for their upcoming season. The teaser features Niall Horan taking fans to the set, prior to filming blind auditions. Fans cannot wait to see the former One Direction member member on the series.

Niall Horan Shows The Set of The Voice Season 23

The blind auditions for season 23 of The Voice is underway and the newest addition to the coaching panel, Niall Horan teases the fans with a studio tour. After a series of cryptic posts from Horan, Blake Shelton, Kelly Clarkson, and Chance the Rapper, The Voice revealed that they are indeed the coaches for season 23.

Horan and Grammy Award-winning artist Chance the Rapper will be first-time coaches on the upcoming season of the series. Clarkson will be a returning coach after sitting out the previous season, and Shelton announced that this will be his last.

“I’m excited to be joining this season of The Voice as a coach. I’m looking forward to meeting and mentoring the new crop of talent while we battle it out with the other teams.” Horan said

Fans have reacted positively to the new coaches, with some joking that they would apply for auditions just to see Horan. Fans are not the only ones excited about Horan being a coach, fellow one direction member Louis Tomlinson also shows his support.

Louis Tomlinson Supports Former His Bandmate

Horan’s former One Direction bandmate has been very vocal in showing his support for the new coach. Tomlinson has admittedly never watched The Voice and its 22 seasons but promised to do so if Horan will be a coach.

Now that the official announcement of Horan has been released, Tomlinson went to do an impromptu Q&A with the fans on Twitter. The fans clamored for Tomlinson to be Horan’s battle advisor to which the singer misinterpreted as being a guest judge on the show.

One direction first made waves around the globe in 2010 becoming quite possibly the most famous boy band until their disbandment in 2016. The members each went on to have successful solo careers of their own, showing support for each other.
