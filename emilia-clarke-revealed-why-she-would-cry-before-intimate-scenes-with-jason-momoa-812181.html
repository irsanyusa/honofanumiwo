Emilia Clarke Revealed Why She Would Cry Before Intimate Scenes With Jason Momoa

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

The phenomenon that was Game of Thrones left an indelible mark on the entertainment landscape, captivating an entire generation of viewers with its intricate storytelling and memorable characters. Among these characters, Emilia Clarke’s portrayal of Daenerys Targaryen, the iconic Mother of Dragons, stood out as a beacon of strength and complexity. Clarke’s ascent to fame was meteoric, and her name became synonymous with the character she brought to life. However, beneath the surface of her success lay a revelation that had a profound impact on her perspective.

Emilia Clarke’s journey as Daenerys Targaryen was a transformative experience that thrust her into the global spotlight. As the silver-haired queen with a quest for power, Clarke’s portrayal resonated deeply with audiences, elevating her to the status of a household name. Her ability to convey Daenerys’s evolution from a vulnerable exile to a determined leader earned her critical acclaim and adoration from fans worldwide. The raw emotion she infused into the character’s struggles and triumphs made her performance an integral part of the show’s appeal.

While the show’s popularity undeniably bolstered Clarke’s career, it also carried unexpected consequences. Joining the sprawling fantasy series brought into focus a previously unrecognized flaw within her character’s trajectory. As Daenerys’s journey unfolded, her storyline took a dark and controversial turn, leading her character down a path of tyranny and violence. This shift in narrative direction presented a challenge for both the viewers and the actors.

For Clarke, the challenge was twofold. On one hand, she had to grapple with the moral complexity of her character’s actions, and on the other, she had to reconcile her own identity with the character she portrayed. The transition from portraying a beloved and empowered queen to embodying a ruler who embraced cruelty was a tumultuous one. This internal conflict illuminated a significant aspect of her character that she had not fully recognized before—the capacity for darkness within even the most righteous of intentions.

This realization opened Clarke’s eyes to the intricacies of human nature and the duality that exists within us all. It highlighted the fine line between heroism and villainy, demonstrating how circumstances and choices can shape a person’s destiny. Clarke’s ability to convey this complexity through her performance showcased her growth as an actress and her willingness to embrace the challenges of her craft.

The dichotomy between the admiration for her performance and the moral quandaries presented by her character’s actions led to conversations that extended beyond the confines of the television screen. Game of Thrones became a cultural touchstone for discussing the blurred boundaries of morality and power. Clarke’s openness about the internal struggles she faced while portraying Daenerys added depth to these conversations, inviting viewers to explore the shades of gray in the human experience.

In hindsight, the flaw within Daenerys’s character served as a catalyst for Emilia Clarke’s personal and professional evolution. It demonstrated her ability to confront difficult themes and navigate complex emotions, both on and off the screen. Her willingness to acknowledge the moral imperfections of her character displayed a level of maturity that resonated with fans and critics alike. The popularity of Game of Thrones and Emilia Clarke’s portrayal of Daenerys Targaryen remains a significant chapter in the history of television. The undeniable allure of the series captivated a generation, while Clarke’s portrayal of the Mother of Dragons left an indelible mark on her career. The revelation of a flaw within her character’s trajectory, while challenging, became a catalyst for growth and introspection. By embracing the complexity of her character’s journey, Clarke not only showcased her prowess as an actress but also engaged audiences in meaningful conversations about the shades of gray that define the human experience.

She had no idea about a big part of her role, but she handled it like a pro.

Emilia Clarke, the acclaimed actress renowned for her iconic role in the highly acclaimed series Game of Thrones, has taken a courageous step by candidly revealing her personal journey through intimate scenes shared with fellow actor Jason Momoa. During a recent podcast conversation, Clarke unveiled a side of her experiences that had remained hidden from the public eye—her tendency to become overwhelmed by emotions, even to the point of shedding tears, before filming these emotionally charged scenes. This revelation offers a poignant glimpse into the challenges she confronted while embodying her character, Daenerys Targaryen, in the hit series.

Moreover, Clarke’s admission exposes a significant layer of her trajectory within the show, as she initially accepted the role without being aware of the explicit content present in the scripts. This unexpected revelation marked a turning point for the actress, sparking a deep reflection on her journey. In a subsequent interview, she delved into her mindset at that time, sharing, “When I accepted the role and subsequently received the scripts, I was confronted with an unforeseen reality—an element that I hadn’t been prepared for.”

By openly addressing the internal struggles and revelations she faced, Emilia Clarke not only provides fans with a rare glimpse into the backstage challenges of a beloved series but also underscores the critical importance of informed decision-making within the entertainment industry. Her willingness to expose her vulnerabilities adds layers of depth to her portrayal of the character and stands as a testament to gracefully navigating unexpected situations with resilience.

In a world where actors often remain silent about the emotional toll that their roles can take, Clarke’s candor shines brightly. Her honesty humanizes the larger-than-life characters she brings to life and bridges the gap between the actor and the audience. The revelation of her pre-scene emotional turmoil serves as a reminder that the creation of art is a deeply personal process, and the emotions experienced by actors are genuine and profound.

As an inspiration to her peers in the industry, Emilia Clarke’s journey is now more relatable and encouraging. Her ability to grapple with unforeseen challenges and integrate them into her performances speaks volumes about her dedication to her craft. It also emphasizes the power of vulnerability, as sharing one’s struggles can create connections that resonate with countless others who may be facing similar experiences. Emilia Clarke’s willingness to peel back the curtain on her emotional journey through intimate scenes not only deepens our appreciation for her work but also reinforces the idea that the human experience, even in the realm of entertainment, is marked by complexities, growth, and the courage to confront the unexpected head-on.

Although Emilia Clarke initially harbored reservations about the explicit scenes in Game of Thrones, she approached her role with a professional mindset, especially considering her recent graduation from drama school. Despite her concerns, she held the belief that if the intimate scenes were integral to the script, they were essential for the narrative. Fortunately, her on-screen counterpart, Jason Momoa, who portrayed her ill-fated husband Khal Drogo, played a pivotal role in easing the situation for her.

Emilia Clarke’s dedication to her craft shone through as she navigated the challenges posed by her character’s storyline. Jason Momoa exhibited remarkable thoughtfulness throughout the entire filming process.

Emilia Clarke also shed light on Jason Momoa’s incredible support and compassion during the filming of the show. While discussing some of the more challenging scenes they had to film together, Clarke revealed that Momoa’s considerate behavior made a significant impact on her experience.

Clarke’s portrayal of Daenerys often required her to engage in emotionally intense and physically demanding scenes. Jason Momoa, who played Khal Drogo, her character’s husband, played a crucial role in helping her navigate through these tough moments. Emilia expressed her gratitude for his kindness and his seasoned experience in the entertainment industry, which helped her feel at ease on set, especially during scenes that pushed her limits. Emilia appreciates how considerate and caring the Aquaman star was toward her as a fellow human being. She said, “He was crying more than I was,” and she feels fortunate to have had such a supportive co-star.

What stood out even more was Momoa’s unwavering commitment to Emilia’s well-being. In one instance, when the weather turned chilly between takes, Momoa took it upon himself to ensure that Clarke was comfortable. He went so far as to request a robe to keep her warm during breaks, showcasing his attentiveness to her needs. This small yet thoughtful gesture speaks volumes about his character and the kind of co-star he was.

Emilia said, “Because Jason had experience—he was an experienced actor who had done a bunch of stuff before coming on to this—he was like, ’Sweetie, this is how it’s meant to be, this is how it’s not meant to be. He was always like, ’Can we get her a robe? She’s shivering!’. He was so kind and considerate and cared about me as a human being.”

This level of emotional investment not only contributed to the authenticity of their on-screen chemistry but also solidified their strong friendship off-screen.

Jason stood by Emilia when she went through her health scare.

In 2019, Emilia Clarke candidly shared her harrowing journey of battling for her life following an aneurysm she experienced after wrapping up the first season of Game of Thrones. Her openness about this challenging period in her life shed light on the resilience and strength that defined her recovery process.

When the topic was broached with Jason Momoa, his response revealed a deep connection to Emilia’s ordeal. He expressed, “I’ve been a part of that entire situation for a very long time. We’ve faced so many moments of uncertainty, and what strikes me is her incredible courage in raising awareness and making a positive impact on the world. We came close to losing her on multiple occasions. My affection for her knows no bounds, and I’m confident she’ll go on to achieve remarkable feats and impart valuable lessons to the world.”

In a heartfelt gesture of their enduring friendship, Momoa shared an image of their reunion, accompanied by a touching caption. He wrote, “There’s an indescribable, wild affection I have for this extraordinary woman. It’s as though we’re enveloped in an aura of laughter and joy every time we’re in each other’s company. While it’s regrettable that our opportunities to meet are limited, the moments we share are as profound as a boundless love. Always and forever, you remain my ’KHAL.’ Love you eternally. Yer Jalan Atthirari Anni.” Translated from Dothraki, this phrase means “moon of my life.”
