'DWTS' Winner Jordan Fisher Details How He Bonds With His Son Through Music

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, June 8, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent Dancing With The Stars

Photo by Bruce Glikas/WireImage

Reading Time: 2 minutes

In an interview at the 2023 Global Citizen Festival in New York City, Actor and Dancing With The Stars Season 25 winner, Jordan Fisher opened up about his special bond with his 15-month-old son, Riley. The young father revealed that Riley’s love for music has become a central part of their relationship.

Jordan Fisher Shares The Intimate Way He Bonds With His Son

At the 2023 Global Citizen’s Festival in New York City, Fisher couldn’t help but share his excitement about Riley’s fascination with music. He explained that Riley has a unique expression when he’s absorbed in a melody. Fisher mimicked his son’s intense focus, painting a vivid picture of their shared moments.

Riley’s journey into the world of walking has been a recent milestone. Like most toddlers, he seeks a helping hand from his parents. Fisher humorously revealed that Riley can walk and run independently, yet he prefers the comfort of his dad’s finger for support. Without it, he quickly descends to the ground.

Looking ahead, Fisher is resolute in his commitment to supporting Riley’s dreams and passions. He wants his son, as well as any future children, to explore their callings freely. The family is ready to provide opportunities for success, learning, and even the occasional stumble along the way.

Fisher also shared a glimpse of his approach to balancing his career and family life. He has a personal rule, if a work trip lasts more than two weeks, his family joins him. Fisher stressed the importance of having his loved ones close, as they serve as his emotional anchor.

Jordan Fisher Reveals His Wife’s New Hobby

Fisher took to Instagram to reveal a newfound hobby of his wife, Ellie Woods. In a video posted on his Instagram account, Fisher shared an exciting update about Ellie’s unexpected venture into the world of gaming.

He goes on to share the fascinating transformation he witnessed in his wife during their time in New York City. Saying that Woods wants to play the horror game, Until Dawn, It’s clear from his tone that he is both amazed and proud of Ellie’s newfound interest.

“She never played a game in her life. Never, ever, ever played a video game. She didn’t grow up around it,” he said. “Her brother never played video games.”

Fisher then describes the moment he stumbled upon Ellie engrossed in her gaming session, a sight that left him utterly surprised and moved. The fact that Ellie, who had never played a video game before, had embraced gaming so wholeheartedly was a delightful shock to Fisher.
