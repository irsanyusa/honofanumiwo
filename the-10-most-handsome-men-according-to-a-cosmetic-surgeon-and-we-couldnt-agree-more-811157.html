The 10 Most Handsome Men According to a Cosmetic Surgeon, and We Couldnt Agree More

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, May 20, 2024

Dr. Julian De Silva, a celebrity facial plastic surgeon, has found the 10 most handsome and attractive men by using the Golden Ratio. The notion examines the symmetry of a person’s face and assesses their visual attractiveness. Let’s take a look at our lovely celebrity ranking today and see if you agree with the doctor.

10. Dwayne Johnson

According to science, Dwayne Johnson is the 10th most handsome man in the world, with an 86.07% score.

9. Henry Golding

Henry Golding got an 87.98% percent in terms of the Golden Ratio. “These brand-new computer mapping techniques allow us to solve some of the mysteries of what it is that makes someone physically beautiful, and the technology is useful when planning patients’ surgeries,” said the doc.

8. George Clooney

7. Chris Evans

Chris Evans is the 7th most handsome man in the world with a score of 91.9% in the Golden Ratio rating.

6. Robert Pattinson

Harry Potter star Robert Pattinson came in sixth, with 92.15%.

5. Jude Bellingham

Jude Bellingham, the 19-year-old England World Cup football sensation, placed fifth with 92.22%.

4. Harry Styles

According to what the new technology says, singer Harry Styles is the fourth most handsome man in the world, with 92.30%.

3. Michael B Jordan

As we inch closer to a perfect score, Black Panther hunk Michael B Jordan was third with a 93.46% score.

1. Regé-Jean Page
