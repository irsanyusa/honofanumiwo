America’s Got Talent: Fantasy League has shared another early release performance from this week’s new episode of the show. It features AGT Season 17 contestants The Pack Drumline, who are competing on judge Simon Cowell’s dream team.

The Pack Drumline Performs in AGT: Fantasy League Early Release

The Pack Drumline, a percussion group from Chicago, competed on Season 17 of AGT, reaching the Semifinals. Now they’re back for AGT: Fantasy League, competing on Cowell’s dream team. Their early release performance had the judges praising their talent.

The group performed “We Will Rock You” by Queen and “Humble” by Kendrick Lamar. They showed off some acrobatic moves to go along with the music, including a couple of backflips. The performance ended with two drummers hanging upside down from other group members’ shoulders as they played.

The judges and audience couldn’t stop smiling throughout the group’s performance. All four judges gave them a standing ovation when it was over. They had some seriously positive feedback to share with The Pack Drumline afterward.

What Did the Judges Think of the Performance?

Judge Howie Mandel called The Pack Drumline’s performance “a Super Bowl moment,” adding, “That is better than anything you have done before on the GT stage, and you are, in my mind tonight, the one to beat.”

Mel B said she was “really annoyed at myself, because you’re on Simon’s dream team, and you just killed it.” Cowell himself went on to wax poetic about the group’s talent, predicting that they’ll win the entire show.

“There are occasions with an artist where you look into their eyes, and you see steel. That, to me, is the sign of a fighter and a star,” Cowell said. “There are other artists who are favorites to win, but I have a feeling that you are gonna surprise everyone and win this whole show.”

You can watch the latest episode of AGT: Fantasy League Monday night at 8/7c on NBC.
