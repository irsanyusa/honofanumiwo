Best Places To Buy Bridesmaid Dresses Online for Any Budget

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, July 26, 2024

Finding great options for your bridal party to wear shouldn’t fill you with dread, or require a trip to a boutique where you may get the hard-sell for a dress your bridesmaids can’t wear again. We’ve rounded up the best online retailers selling bridesmaid dresses, from fashionable designer frocks to simple, airy shifts just made for a beachy bridal party.

Our top picks for the best places to buy bridesmaids dresses online

Anthropologie

Anthropologie

There’s something for every kind of nuptial, from a beachy casual destination to a formal black tie during the holidays in Anthropologie’s bridal party collection. From elegant and eclectic slinky sheaths to fun and fairy tale-worthy tulle gowns, these bridesmaid dresses are guaranteed to make an impression at the altar and the afterparty. And because Anthropologie knows how to curate flattering gowns, your bridesmaids will wear them again. Shopping tip: Anthropologie also made our list of the best places to buy a wedding dress online.

Specifications:

  • Size range: Varies on the dress; a majority are 00-26, and some plus sizes are available
  • Shipping: Free standard shipping for items over $50
  • Returns: 30-day online or in-store returns
  • Prices starting at: Varies on the dress, some as low as $125. At the time of publication, there were eight dresses available under $200

What we like:

  • Curated shop
  • Diverse options
  • Designs stray from typical bridal attire

What we don’t like:

  • Some dresses are not size inclusive

J.Crew

J.Crew Collection Layered Slip Dress in Sheer Organza

J.Crew doesn’t sell wedding gowns anymore, but it still carries a chic line of dresses suitable for bridesmaids and other wedding party members. The J.Crew Wedding Shop offers a range of elegant floor-length slip dresses, designed with everything from sequins to floral crepe. Its mini-slip dress in layered organza or cotton poplin is charming and simple, but stylish enough for the ceremony.

Note, these are suggestions by the brand to use as bridal party attire, but J.Crew’s affordable designs won’t disappoint the bride or her bridesmaids. (Or even the groom and his groomsmen—J.Crew made our list of the best men’s clothing brands.)

Specifications:

  • Size range: 00-24; 3X
  • Shipping: 3 to 6 business days (standard)
  • Returns: Free returns for up to 30 days in-store or online
  • Prices starting at: $98

What we like:

  • Classic lines
  • Wide range of designs and colors for slip dresses
  • Matching shoes and purses available for purchase

What we don’t like:

  • Many designs are slip dresses that don’t flatter every figure
  • Would be nice to have more choices

Nordstrom

If you want a brick-and-mortar option in your back pocket while you shop online for bridesmaid dresses, it’s worth ducking (virtually or

physically) into a Nordstrom store. There, you will find beautiful gowns and frocks (from designers like Badgley Mischka and Shona Joy) galore to fit all your bridesmaids. Seasonal, stylish looks abound at Nordstrom, and because of its large inventory (over 200 bridesmaid dresses), it is easy to check out a few styles and compare them. Bonus: If you live near one of 300-plus Nordstrom stores, they offer to tailor when you purchase your bridal attire online or in person. It’s also no surprise Nordstrom made our list of the best places to buy dresses online and best places to buy shoes online (check out their formal shoe selection particularly, especially for finding the best wedding shoes).

Specifications:

  • Size range: 00-24, in some designs 28 and 4+
  • Shipping: Free standard shipping
  • Returns: Free returns in-store or online
  • Prices starting at: $50

What we like:

  • Wide selection and price points
  • Free shipping and returns
  • Free tailoring

What we don’t like:

  • The range of gowns for larger sizes is small

Revolve

Symone Dress by Amanda Uprichard

These bridesmaid gowns are stylish and sophisticated, and you can search for dresses based on venues like black tie or beach weddings to find the perfect style for the big day. The best part? This retailer also offers free two-day shipping, so if you’re in a rush, Revolve can solve your time crunch.

Specifications:

What we like:

Don’t forget to browse their accessories section.

What to keep in mind when buying a bridesmaid dress online

When buying bridesmaid dresses online, consider a few factors because nothing is worse than being forced to wear an uncomfortable dress for hours and hours. If you’re lucky, the bride will have jumped on the trend of having the bridesmaids choose their own dress.

Here are some tips on what to look for when buying bridesmaid dresses online:

Style

These days, you don’t have to keep your bridesmaids in the same dress—break with tradition and allow them to choose what they wear. Mismatched or mix-and-match bridesmaid outfits are trending. Let them wear what they want. If you need a bit of fashion cohesion in your wedding party’s attire, you could give them a color or palette.

Measurements

Take careful measurements (bust, hip, and waist, plus sleeves and hollow to hem, i.e., collarbone to where you want the hem to finish) before you buy a dress to find the right size. If your dress is being made to order, you must send your measurements to the designer.

Timeline

It’s extremely stress-inducing (both for you and the bride) to find out your dress will arrive after the wedding. Consider how much time you need to secure your gown and tailor it to fit. Note many made-to-order dresses can take months to create and ship.

Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

When should I order my bridal dress?

Give yourself at least six months if you’re planning to wear a made-to-order dress.

How will I know to order the right size for me?

Like most clothing for special occasions, it’s worth ordering a few sizes to find your best fit, even after you take your measurements. Formalwear can usually benefit from tailoring as well.

What is the average price to spend on a bridesmaid dress?

According to Zola, an online wedding registry, the average bridesmaid dress is around $150, but that doesn’t include the cost of alterations or the shoes and accessories that may have to go with it. (Sidenote: Zola is also a great place to start your registry, and made our list of the best places to buy wedding photo albums.)

Who is supposed to buy the bridesmaid dresses?

Traditionally, the bridesmaids are responsible for buying their dresses. We suggest brides consider allowing their wedding party to have some say on what they’ll spend their money on. Buying a high-quality, well-made dress you love and want to wear again may make it worth the splurge.
