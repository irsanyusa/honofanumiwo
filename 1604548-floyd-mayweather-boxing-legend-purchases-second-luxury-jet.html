Floyd Mayweather: Boxing Legend Purchases Second Luxury Jet

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, June 11, 2024
  • Retired boxing legend Floyd Mayweather has added a second private jet to his collection
  • The 47-year-old is famous for his extravagant spending on luxury goods like cars and watches
  • The American retired from the sport in 2017 following his huge win over MMA legend, Conor McGregor

Retired boxing legend, Floyd "Money" Mayweather recently bought a second private jet and shared the news on Instagram.

The American later shared photos of the jet and how the branding with his TMT logos was put together.

The 47-year-old left the sport of boxing in 2017 with a 50-0 record and has appeared in several exhibition bouts against mixed martial arts MMA stars and YouTubers.

Mayweather is known for flaunting his wealth, including cars, jets, watches, and other luxury items over the years.

His first jet reportedly cost $50 million and is named, 'Air Mayweather', and has a multitude of lavish features thanks to his $703 million net worth, per Daily Star.

His latest Gulfstream jet is top of the range and has been branded with the TMT logo and his name on the side.

According to Private Jet Clubs, the plane features 12 premium leather seats and gold-plated cup holders.

The jet features 12 premium leather seats customized with his logo, gold-plated cup holders, and custom touches.

Mayweather's expensive watch

Mayweather is also among the top celebrities who own the most expensive watches that celebrities wear.

The boxing icon owns a Jacob & Co. watch, which is considered the most expensive watch ever sold. Its worth is estimated at $18 million.
