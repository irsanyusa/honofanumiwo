20+ Things That Fit Together So Perfectly They Look Like Perfection Paradise

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 10, 2024

Just as there are people who notice mistakes in everything they see, there are also those who manage to find the small little details that make everything that surrounds them perfect. Lucky for us, they even go through the trouble of taking a picture to capture that perfection we all sometimes need to remember that everything is connected and life is nothing but a great Tetris game.

Bright Side found a Reddit thread with several examples shared by users of things that fit so well together they can just make your day.

1. “Recently built a garage addition, and the customer’s car just happens to slide right in by chance.”

2. “I was told my washi tapes would fit here.”

3. “Exactly 2 bags of FarmRich mozzarella bites”

4. “Like a glove”

5. “Putting 2 boxes of cereal in my reusable shopping bag”

6. “How the milk and water bottles fit under the desk”

7. “Not an inch more, not an inch less”

8. “Discovered new scrubbing brush fits perfectly under these shelves at work.”

9. “The way my longboard fits into the compartment in my trunk”

10. “Amazon got it perfectly right.”

11. “Went to a restaurant, and I tried to get out of someone’s way and realized this nook was perfect for me.”

12. “Any smaller and my stroopwaffle wouldn’t fit atop my coffee, but if it were any bigger, it wouldn’t evenly heat.”

13. “This cheese was made for this slice of bread”

14. “All of the computers from the entire office fit perfectly into the random crate we use for moving stuff.”

15. “My omelette du fromage accidentally fits my plate.”

16. “My wife’s wedding band inside mine. I guess you say we are a perfect fit... see what I did there?”

17. “As soon as I unwrapped my new soap, I thought there’s no way that’s going to fit.”

18. “New apartment. I guess I picked the right shampoo and conditioner.”

19. “So neat you can only smile”

20. “Years learning helping my dad’s moving company.”

21. “My wife was wearing the right jacket at the right time.”

22. “I took a picture of the London Eye through the window on a boat.”

23. “2 different brands, perfectly match up.”

Which side are you on: perfectionist or messy? What have you discovered in your home or around you that fits perfectly?
