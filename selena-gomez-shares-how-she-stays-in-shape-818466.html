Selena Gomez Shares How She Stays in Shape

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, August 22, 2024

Selena Gomez’s changing body has been grabbing headlines, drawing both praise and criticism. Some have speculated about weight gain or even pregnancy. However, Selena isn’t letting critics get to her. Instead, she’s bravely sharing her tips for staying fit, giving us a glimpse into her journey of self-care and empowerment.

She follows a healthy diet.

The actress’s personal trainer revealed her healthy eating habits, emphasizing lots of veggies and protein. Gomez prefers brown rice for its health benefits over white. It’s been proven that choosing brown rice instead of white rice can offer more nutritional benefits and help you maintain a balanced diet.

During long shows, the actress depends on protein and avocado for sustained energy. Additionally, the trainer shares Gomez’s favorite salad recipe, loaded with chicken, grapes, celery, and hydrating ingredients.

Her fitness routine is diverse.

If you’ve ever tried sticking to a regular workout routine, you know how it can get boring and drain your motivation. Gomez believes in mixing things up in her fitness routines with her trainer to keep them fun. She does various activities like Pilates, hiking, dance cardio, circuit training, yoga, and spinning. Keeping your workouts diverse and interesting is crucial to staying motivated and avoiding burnout.

She likes her shower hot.

Although taking a hot shower for too long might dehydrate your skin, it offers some advantages. Immersing yourself in hot water can effectively ease body tension and soothe muscle fatigue. According to Gomez, she likes to take steamy hot showers to relax after a long day. The actress reveals that she even transforms her shower into a steam room.

She uses mud to keep her body rejuvenated.

The actress keeps her body skin toned with regular care. She uses body scrubs. She also enjoys mud therapy treatments, finding humor in looking like she’s covered in mud but appreciating its skin benefits.

Mud, like clay masks, has antibacterial and exfoliating properties. To finish her routine, she uses body brushing.

She takes care of her mental health.

The actress spoke openly about her mental health struggles following her diagnosis. Yet she found the courage to seek help. Now equipped with the understanding and answers she needed, she confidently discusses her daily efforts. She finds immense joy in cherishing the little things and delights in sharing these moments with her audience.

Just like Selena Gomez, Jennifer Aniston also faces body shaming and even pregnancy rumors. Despite societal pressure and media speculation, Aniston stands strong. She bravely challenges the idea that a woman’s happiness depends on marriage or having children. Rather than giving in to the pressure, she fearlessly confronts these rumors, clearing up misconceptions that have followed her for years.
