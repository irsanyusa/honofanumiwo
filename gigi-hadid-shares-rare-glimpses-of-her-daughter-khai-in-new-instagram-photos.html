Gigi Hadid Shares Rare Glimpses of Her Daughter Khai in New Instagram Photos

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, May 24, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Dimitrios Kambouris/Getty Images for Victoria's Secret

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Gigi Hadid offered fans a rare look at her daughter Khai this week, posting several photos with the three-year-old on Instagram. The images provide a touching glimpse into Hadid’s recent endeavors as well as her life as a mother.

Gigi Hadid Shares Glimpses of Daughter Khai on Instagram

On Wednesday, Hadid shared a variety of snapshots – including modeling pics, bikini shots, and product promotions. However, the highlight of the photos featured Khai, her daughter with ex Zayn Malik.

One image shows Hadid and Khai sitting on a couch by a Christmas-decorated window. Another captures Khai’s small hand as she engages in arts and crafts. There is also a beach shot of Hadid and Khai sitting in the sand.

In a final pic, Khai wears a pink snowsuit while going up a rock-climbing wall. Her face is obscured in keeping with Hadid and Malik’s policy of not showing her on social media. Hadid previously celebrated Khai’s third birthday in September with Instagram tributes.

“Have been celebrating our THREE YR OLD this week… & just so honored to get to be her mama, to get to see life through her !!!! Happy Birthday to my sweetest, smartest, spunkiest bestie !!!!!!!!” she wrote.

Malik also marked the occasion online, calling Khai “my everything” and thanking her for the “love you bring to my life.” According to sources, Hadid and Malik have worked hard to co-parent Khai amicably since their split. Their main priority is providing stability for their little girl.

Though Hadid’s Instagram offers limited looks at her daughter, this week’s posts provide touching insight into her joyful role as Khai’s mother. The images reveal Hadid’s clear adoration for her “bestie” as Khai navigates the magical early years of childhood.

Gigi Hadid Was Recently Spotted at an Airport With Bradley Cooper

Aside from the recent sweet photographs of her daughter Khai, Hadid is making headlines for her budding romance with Bradley Cooper. The supermodel was spotted with Cooper at the airport this week shortly before she posted rare images with her three-year-old on Instagram.

Hadid and Cooper have fueled dating rumors for months, ever since they were first photographed leaving a restaurant together last October. Sources say they’ve been quietly spending more time together, and their relationship appears to be getting serious.

The latest sighting came Tuesday when Hadid and Cooper were seen traveling through a New York City airport terminal together. Hadid wore a bright green hat, while Cooper sported headphones around his neck. At one point, Cooper stopped to embrace a friend in the terminal.

While Hadid treasures privacy, especially concerning Khai, her airport date with Cooper confirms their relationship is going strong. Sources claim they have had months together to build an intimate connection out of the public eye.
