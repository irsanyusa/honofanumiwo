What do cardinals represent? The meaning behind the red bird

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, June 9, 2024

Cardinals, also called red birds, are a species of songbirds, most of which have crested heads and thick bills. People from different cultures associate seeing these birds with various things. What do cardinals represent?

The cardinal is the official bird of seven eastern US states and is particularly common in the Southeast. These birds are often featured on Christmas decorations in snowy scenes. Cardinal symbolism exists worldwide as part of different cultures, religions and traditions.

What do cardinals represent?

A cardinal is thought to hold various meanings. It symbolises energy, security, good fortune, protection, romantic love, willpower and determination. Here is a look at different redbird meanings based on spirituality, love and relationships, positivity and wellness.
