Former America’s Got Talent standout mentalists, Amélie van Tass and Thommy Ten, known as The Clairvoyants, are headed back out on the road for their upcoming Holiday Spectacular Tour. The tour kicks off on November 24, 2023 in South Dakota, and ends on December 30 in Honolulu, Hawaii. Tickets will be available for purchase on June 30.

The Clairvoyants Announce Upcoming Holiday Tour

The Clairvoyants are set to hit the road this holiday season with their brand new holiday-themed show featuring magic tricks, mind-reading, and illusions. The duo will appear alongside their mind-reading dog Koni. Tickets for all dates will go on sale on Friday June, 30 at 10AM local time. Tickets can be purchased at The Clairvoyants’ website.

The duo released a press statement regarding their upcoming shows: “Get ready to witness the mesmerizing wonders and enchantment that await you on our upcoming holiday spectacular tour! We’re beyond thrilled to unveil a magical show like no other, set to dazzle and captivate audiences during the most magical time of the year. We’re ready to showcase a breathtaking array of brand-new acts that will leave you spellbound! From mind reading to awe-inspiring illusions, we’re crafting a performance that will ignite your imagination and transport you to a realm of wonder. And let’s not forget the real star of the show, Mr. Koni Hundini, who will unveil his latest tricks, bringing joy and laughter to all.”

Who Are The Clairvoyants?

For those who may not remember, The Clairvoyants rose to fame after competing on America’s Got Talent season 11. On the show, they were able to wow Simon Cowell, Heidi Klum, Mel B, and Howie Mandel with their mind reading. At the end of the season, they finished in second place behind Grace Vanderwaal. Later, they returned to the AGT stage for America’s Got Talent: The Champions.

Since their time on AGT, The Clairvoyants have continued to perform their full length show worldwide. From 2021 to 2022, the couple performed two shows a night, five days a week as part of the America’s Got Talent Las Vegas LIVE cast.

After the duo departed from AGT’s Las Vegas show, they tied the knot at the famous Little White Chapel. Earlier this year, The Clairvoyants hit the road in Canada, The United Kingdom and The United States.
