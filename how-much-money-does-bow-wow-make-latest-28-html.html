Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 19, 2024
The original name of Bow Wow is Shad Gregory Moss. He was born on March 9, 1987, at Columbus, Ohio, U.S. He is an actor, rapper and television host by profession. He launched his first record, “Beware of Dog”, in 2000 at age 13, which was followed by “Doggy Bag” in 2001. In 2003, Bow Wow launched his third record “Unleashes”. As of September 27, 2015, he has finalized a deal to Bad Boy Records. As of 2018, Bow Wow’s net worth is $2.5 million. Bow Wow created his first film appearance in All About the Benjamins, as a cameo in 2002. In the same season, Bow Wow created his debut as the lead part in Like Mike. He later started to act in films in lead roles, such as Johnson Family Vacation in 2004 and Roll Bounce in 2005. He also performed a supporting part in the film The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift in 2006. He was seen in TV series for five episodes on Entourage and showed up as Brody Nelson in CSI: Cyber until the show’s cancelation in 2016. On Aug 10, 2013, via his formal YouTube page, Bow Wow mentioned the key purpose why Underrated had been late was the outcome of raising his little girl and patiently waiting until he was more mature; he also mentioned he intended on focusing on and launching the record after he would have been 28. On July 24, 2014, Bow Wow declared via Instagram that after the 2014 BET Prizes, he was going by his actual name Shad Moss, declaring that he created a lot of record as Bow Wow and that it was now here we are at “the next section and challenge”. He mentioned that “Bow Wow” no more fit with his new character as he was now a father, actor, host and he had grown up from what he had been in the beginning in his lifestyle. On Aug 7, 2016, declared his retirement from rapping and will launch one more record, named NYLTH, which is tentatively planned for launch in 2017.
